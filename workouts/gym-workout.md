# Gym — Golf Strength & Mobility

## Warm-Up (10 min)
- Bike or treadmill: 5 min
- Hip circles x10 each direction
- Arm swings (hug & open) x10
- Torso rotations x10/side

## Strength (2–3 rounds)
- Cable rotations (light) x10/side
- Goblet squat to bench x10
- Single-arm cable row x10/side
- Pallof press x12/side

## Core & Balance
- Dead bug or bird dog x8–10/side
- Single-leg balance 30 sec/side

## Cooldown
- Hip flexor stretch
- Hamstrings
- Thoracic opener
